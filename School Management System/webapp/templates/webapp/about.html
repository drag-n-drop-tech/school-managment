About Us

“Dedication to academic excellence our innovative approach to learning and our passion for nurturing creativity”

Gurukula international school was founded in 2005 with Montessori, primary and higher primary system of education. It is situated near banavara, Arsikere Tq., Hassan District. Surrounded with 6 acre with large playground and beautiful garden with peaceful atmosphere. As a feature it is affiliated to CBSE with residential and have conducive environment for learning.

The system of education for tiny-tots and young children has been widely recognized as an ideal system to guide the children in their primary age and prepare for them for higher education and to make them good citizen of the country.

We have track record of cent percent result right from the inception a large number of them getting through with distinction. Seven batch of X std students of our GIs went out leaving behind strong foot marks.

Our Mission

To nurture individual talents and abilities empowering all children and help them to develop into creative, disciplined, dynamic leaders of the future. These children will be active global citizens contributing richly to our nation and the world. To raise them as independent, responsible, resourceful and successful individuals with high self-esteem and confidence.

Our Vision

Our vision is to foster a society of individual capable of progressive thinking, creating and initiating positive change in the social, economic and cultural fabric to impact the global community through knowledge, understanding and attitude.

Our objectives

Fostering in students a sense of pride in our culture and heritage. Exposure to knowledge among students through Curricular and extra-Curricular activities. To develop the intrinsic qualities of the students. To mould students into good citizens through morals, education discipline and encouragement

School Responsibility

  • Quality Education with discipline
  • Concern about each child
  • Individual personalized attention and focusing on each and every child
  • We will conduct career counseling every year for out going grade 10th std students
  • Emphasis on life skills that go beyond their curriculum
