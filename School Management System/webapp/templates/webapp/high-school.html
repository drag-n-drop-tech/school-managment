High School

High school is a kind of school, a place where people go to learn skills for future jobs… a high school is a school that students go to usually for grades 9 through 12, from the ages of about 14-15 to about 17-18.

It is also the last school that we requires the student to go to. We provide support and guidance to our students as they grow, and set clear expectations for behavior so they can grow up into respected global citizens.

Our diverse school community and our teaching encourage them to respect all cultures, and to see the world as a beautiful place of opportunity.
