Pre-Primary

Nursery school provides the building blocks for numeracy and literacy learning, we prepare children to be emotionally ready for primary school. In the nursery children are taught how to interact with each other and with adults in an appropriate way.

We have monthly themes, weekly topics and everyday activities for better focus. They will also have art, music, drama hours with interactive sessions to help them appreciate different aspects of learning.

The kindergarten program consists of M1,M2 and M3, it is designed to equip the child with activity based learning modules with introduction to writing, reading, maths, languages and every day skills in a motivated environment. It is the basic introduction to structured class sessions that will ensure their smooth transition to primary school.

Creating a competitive physical environment for better learning has always been our goal and we keep them coming. Come let us join hand together to mold a better tomorrow.
